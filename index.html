Biography

Deepika Padukone(pronounced
[d̪i:pɪka: pəɖʊko:ɳe:];
born 5 January 1986) is an Indian actress
who works predominantly in Hindi films.
She is India's highest-paidactress,
as of 2023,and her accolades include
three Filmafare Awards. She features in
listings of the nation's most popular
personalities;Time named her one of the 100
most influential people in the world in 2018
=and awarded her the Time100 Impact Award in 2022.
